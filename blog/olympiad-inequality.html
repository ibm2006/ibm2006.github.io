Olympiad Inequality

Initial notes

Olympiad inequalities are often less about memorizing famous inequalities and more about recognizing structure. A good solution usually explains why the expression has the right form, where equality can occur, and which transformation makes the hidden symmetry visible.

Techniques to Organize

  • AM-GM, Cauchy-Schwarz, Jensen, Holder, and rearrangement.
  • Homogenization and normalization such as setting a sum equal to 1.
  • Substitution methods for symmetric expressions.
  • Checking equality cases before choosing the main inequality.

Questions for Future Notes

  • When does uvw reduce a symmetric inequality to two variables?
  • How can tangent line methods make Jensen-style arguments clearer?
  • What are reliable ways to avoid overusing AM-GM?
